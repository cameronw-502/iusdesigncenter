Designing for a better NOW.

We provide community-focused design solutions by catering to campus programs and organizations, as well as community non-profit and charitable commercial projects.

Indiana University Southeast Design Center consists of Graphic Design Students working in a real Design Center environment.

Design Center is an in-house non-profit graphic design class at Indiana University Southeast. A component of the Fine Arts program, Design Center is housed in the School of Arts and Letters and provides a variety of print and web-based design serves to clients both on and off campus.
Directed by a faculty advisor, students are able to work in a real-life agency setting that provides them with the experiences they will need before they enter the job market. Team members work directly with clients, individually and as teams, through all phases of front-end production work.
Team members hail from a variety of campus programs including: Fine Arts, Informatics, Computer Science, and the IMP, supporting degree tracks including B.A., B.F.A., B.S., and B.A.S. Enrollees have the opportunity to receive intern credit from IU Southeastâ€™s department of Career Services.
